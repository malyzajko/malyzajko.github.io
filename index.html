I am an associate professor at the Dept. of Information Technology at Uppsala University, and adjunct faculty at MPI-SWS. Previously, I was a tenure-track faculty at MPI-SWS, and before that, I did my PhD with Viktor Kuncak at EPFL. I am generally interested in programming languages, software verification, program synthesis and approximate computing. Currently, my focus lies on applications in numerical and embedded domains.

Projects

  • Blossom - a guided blackbox fuzzer to infer numerical kernel ranges
  • Lassie - HOL4 tactics by example
  • Pine - synthesizer for floating-point numerical loops
  • LTLTalk - instructing robots by example
  • Incarnate - PL Tools and Techniques for 3D Printing
  • Icing - a nondeterministic floating-point source semantics
  • Flipper - a natural language robot interface
  • FloVer - a certificate checker for roundoff error bounds
  • Daisy - a framework for accuracy analysis and synthesis of numerical programs
  • Rosa - the real compiler
